--- Prefill Path Analysis (theoretical) ---
Dispatch reduction per forward_prefill call:
  Pattern 1 (copy+norm → copy_and_rms_norm_batch): 24 layers × 1 saved = 24
  Pattern 2 (add+copy+norm → fused_residual_add_norm_batch): 24 layers × 2 saved = 48
  Pattern 3 (add+copy → add_and_copy): 24 layers × 1 saved = 24
  Total: 96 fewer Metal dispatches per prefill

Memory bandwidth reduction:
  Each fused kernel eliminates 1-2 extra buffer read/write passes per row.
  For hidden=1536, 24 layers: ~72KB fewer memory passes per token in batch.

Impact: Prefill latency improvement proportional to dispatch overhead fraction.
At ~3µs/dispatch average: ~288µs saved per prefill (0.1-0.5% depending on seq_len).
Larger impact on short prefills where dispatch overhead dominates.
